Thanaka Product

Thanaka, a cosmetic paste made from the bark of the thanaka tree, is a long-standing Burmese tradition used for both health and beauty purposes. It's known for its ability to protect and beautify the skin, with applications ranging from sun protection to anti-aging and acne treatment.

Beauty and Cosmetic Uses:
Sun Protection:
Thanaka paste is a natural sunscreen, protecting the skin from the sun's harmful UV rays.
Skincare:
Thanaka is believed to help with acne, rashes, and blemishes, and can promote smooth, healthy skin.
